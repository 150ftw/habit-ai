HABIT AI

The Procrastination vs Productivity AI Simulator

HABIT AI is a Reinforcement Learning (RL) simulation platform designed to visualize the delicate balance between mental states and professional output. Using a human-like heuristic agent, it demonstrates how Energy, Motivation, and Workload interact to create long-term behavioral patterns.

Watch how different "Personalities" respond to a simulated Monday-Friday work week, or take manual control to test your own productivity strategies.
